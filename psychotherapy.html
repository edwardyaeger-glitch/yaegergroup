Psychotherapy

Private, long-term clinical work for professionals in demanding environments.

Psychotherapy requires more than education, training, and experience. It requires judgment, restraint, and the capacity to tolerate complexity without rushing to resolution.

Our clinical work is grounded in licensed training and conducted within ethical and professional boundaries. We are attentive to role clarity and do not blur psychotherapy with advisory, consulting, or performance-oriented work. What differentiates the work is not a particular technique or orientation, but the way responsibility is held: we attend closely to context, personal, relational, cultural, and professional, without collapsing the work into problem-solving or reassurance.

We work deliberately and selectively. Not every concern requires therapy, and not every therapeutic approach is a good fit.

For more information and to get started, visit The Yaeger Practice.
